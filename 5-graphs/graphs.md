## Graphs

A graph is a data structure made up of nodes (like list nodes and tree nodes), where the nodes may be connected by pointers.

1. Matrix
2. Adjacency Matrix
3. Adjacency List

Linked lists and Trees are also Graphs. The nodes are also called as Vertices. 
The connection to these vertices (i.e one vertex to another) are call Edges.
Graphs have cycles. But linked list and trees do not.

Edges <= Vertices**2

* **Directional graph**:  edges have direction
* **Undirected graph**: edges can be bidirectional
